Disney Explorer

Social media for frequent Disney travelers.

Open Web App

Disney Explorer is a social media app that allows you to give and gather information about Disney through fellow Disney travelers. Connect with people who share the same interests as you and your family. Share your experience by posting about certain events or attractions. Check-in a share your trip with people outisde of Disney Explorer.

Check-in and Share!
As you make friends throughout your trip, you can add them on Disney Explorer to keep in touch! You'll be able to send messages directly to them to make sure they're having a good time!
Let others know about your Disney experience. Share with other Disney goers the ins and outs of Disney theme parks to make their experience more enjoyable.

Design process

Interview with Sarah Harris

How often does you and your family attend Disney?
We use to go every other year but recently it's been once a year.

Around what time do you start planning your trip compared to the date of the actual trip?
I'd say probably around eight to six months before the trip.

Insight Statements:
Families need guidnance before and while they attend Disney.
Families have better experiences when they have researched their Disney plans.
People draw inspiration from others when they've seen that the person's idea was a success.

How Might We...
-Create a platform where families can receive guidance from others who attend Disney.
-Have multiple ideas from various people regarding information about Disney attractions.
-create a sharing space where people can draw inspiration and become creative with their Disney experience.

Design Question
How might a social media type platform help improve the experience of those who attend Disney?
Persona
Susan is a 33 year old mother of two. She works as a bank teller at their local bank and is thankful to have time off to spend vacation with her family. Susan and her husband, George, are taking their 5 year old daughter, Caroline, and their 7 year old son, Lucas, to Disney for the first time.
As Susan is planning their vacation she starts to wonder which attractions would be the best use of their time and most enjoyable for their children. She begins looking for reviews on Disney and see an advertisment for Disney Explorer.
Using the application, she finds helpful reviews regarding how some attractions have a longer wait time at certain times of the day. She also finds insider deals on tickets through the application.
